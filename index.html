Easy lookup web accessibility guidelines

We want the process of making an accessible website easier. These guidelines contain all of the criteria that we feel suffice to ensure a website can be used by every kind of user. It's aligned closely with the WCAG 2.0 spec*, includes some of RNIB's Surf Right standard and some of our own too.

Search by priority level, department or keyword and get a succinct explanation of the relevant criteria. Comments and feedback welcome.
